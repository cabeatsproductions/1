Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how C / A Beats Creator Upload Interface handles creator authentication, user-controlled publishing actions, and limited account-related information when using TikTok official systems and integrations.

1. Overview

C / A Beats Creator Upload Interface is a creator-focused publishing interface developed by C / A Beats & Productions.

The platform allows creators to authenticate using TikTok official OAuth systems and manually publish content using TikTok official APIs and user-controlled workflows.

User-Controlled Actions

The platform does not perform automated publishing, unattended posting, or background uploads. All publishing actions require direct user interaction and confirmation.

2. Information We May Access

When users authenticate through TikTok official systems, limited account-related information may be temporarily accessed depending on the permissions approved by the user.

  • Public TikTok profile information
  • Creator nickname and profile identifiers
  • Video publishing permissions approved by the user
  • Visibility and interaction preferences selected during publishing

3. TikTok Authentication

Authentication is handled using TikTok OAuth and TikTok official authorization systems.

User login credentials are not stored or managed directly by C / A Beats Creator Upload Interface.

Users control authorization permissions directly through TikTok official consent screens.

4. Content Publishing

Content publishing actions occur only after direct user initiation and explicit confirmation.

Users manually:

  • Select videos for upload
  • Write captions and metadata
  • Select visibility preferences
  • Choose interaction settings
  • Confirm publishing actions

No Background Automation

The platform does not publish content automatically without direct user action.

5. Data Usage

Information accessed through TikTok official systems is used solely to provide creator publishing functionality and platform operations requested directly by the user.

The platform does not sell personal information or creator account information to third parties.

6. Data Storage

Uploaded content and temporary publishing-related data may be processed for operational purposes required to complete publishing actions initiated by the user.

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect operational data and creator account access.

7. User Rights

Users maintain control over their TikTok account permissions and can revoke access through TikTok account settings at any time.

Users may also discontinue use of the platform at any time.
